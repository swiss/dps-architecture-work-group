Eurostat Data Catalogue ()
Description: The EUROSTAT data catalogue is a comprehensive repository of statistical data and indicators maintained by EUROSTAT, the statistical office of the European Union (EU). It serves as a central platform for accessing a wide range of statistical information about the EU and its member states. The catalogue provides a structured and organized collection of datasets covering various domains such as economy, population, employment, agriculture, trade, and many others.
