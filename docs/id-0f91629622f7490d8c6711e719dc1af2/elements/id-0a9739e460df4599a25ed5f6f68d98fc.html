Data Quality ()
Definition: Data Quality ABB is a Business Service that enables the implementation of an organization's data quality management capabilities, provisioned via a data quality service delivery mode by, or on behalf of an organization. A Data Quality Business Service comprises any data quality service exposed to ensure the accuracy, completeness, reliability, and timeliness of data within the organization.
