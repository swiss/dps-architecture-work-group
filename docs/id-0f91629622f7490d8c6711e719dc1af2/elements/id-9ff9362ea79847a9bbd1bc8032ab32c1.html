Survey Form ()
Definition: The Survey Form is designed for conducting detailed surveys or polls. It can encompass a variety of question types, including multiple-choice, Likert scales, and open-ended responses. This form is used to gather data on customer preferences, market trends, or public opinions, providing valuable insights that can guide business strategies, marketing efforts, and product development.
