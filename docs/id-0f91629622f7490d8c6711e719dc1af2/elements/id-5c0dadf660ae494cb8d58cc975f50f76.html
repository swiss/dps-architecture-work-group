ESPD-EDM ()
Description: The European Single Procurement Document (ESPD) is a standardized form used in public procurement processes within the European Union (EU). As a data object, the ESPD represents a structured document that contains essential information about a supplier or contractor. It includes details such as the company's identification, financial standing, technical and professional abilities, and any legal grounds for exclusion. The ESPD serves as a preliminary self-declaration by suppliers, allowing them to demonstrate their eligibility and suitability to participate in public procurement procedures across EU member states.
