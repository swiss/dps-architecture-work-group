Digital Certification eSignature Service ()
Definition: The Digital Certification eSignature Service enables users to securely sign electronic documents using digital certificates. It leverages cryptographic techniques to ensure the integrity, authenticity, and non-repudiation of digitally signed documents. This service provides a user-friendly interface accessible via the web, allowing users to upload their documents, apply their digital signature, and generate a digitally signed version. It utilizes digital certificate authorities to issue and manage digital certificates, providing a trusted framework for electronic signatures. The Digital Certification eSignature Service enhances document security, eliminates the need for physical signatures, and supports the digital transformation of business processes.
