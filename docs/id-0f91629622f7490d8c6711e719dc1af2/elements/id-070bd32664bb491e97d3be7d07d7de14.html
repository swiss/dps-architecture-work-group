Analytical Capacity ()
Definition: The Analytical Capacity Value ABB refers to the ability of public administrations to gather, analyse, and utilize data and evidence in decision-making processes. It involves having the skills, tools, and institutional arrangements to collect, interpret, and apply data to inform policy development, program design, and performance monitoring.
