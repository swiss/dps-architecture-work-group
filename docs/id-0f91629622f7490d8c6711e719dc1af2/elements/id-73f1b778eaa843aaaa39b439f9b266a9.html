Gaia-X ()
Description: Gaia-X establish the principles, rules, and standards for participating in the European data infrastructure initiative. These agreements include a participation agreement, data sovereignty agreement, interoperability agreement, security and privacy agreement, governance agreement, and compliance agreement. They ensure that participants maintain control over their data, promote interoperability, prioritize security and privacy, establish governance structures, and adhere to relevant laws and regulations. Together, these agreements enable secure and sovereign data sharing within the Gaia-X ecosystem while fostering trust and collaboration among participants. Additional information: Gaia-X is a European initiative aimed at creating a federated data infrastructure to promote secure and sovereign data sharing among businesses, organizations, and individuals. The Agreements on the Use of Common Infrastructure of Gaia-X outline the principles, rules, and standards that govern the participation and utilization of the Gaia-X infrastructure.
