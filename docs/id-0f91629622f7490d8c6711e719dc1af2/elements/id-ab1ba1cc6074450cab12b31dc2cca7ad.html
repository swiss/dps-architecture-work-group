Financial Management ()
Definition: The Financial Management Application Service refers to a software service that facilitates the management and processing of financial operations and transactions within an organization. It encompasses a range of functionalities, including budgeting, accounting, financial planning, reporting, invoicing, and payment processing. The service streamlines financial processes, ensures accurate recording and tracking of financial data, and provides insights into the organization's financial performance.
