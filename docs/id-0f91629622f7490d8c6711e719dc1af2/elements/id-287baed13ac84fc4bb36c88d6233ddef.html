Business Intelligence ()
Definition: Business Intelligence ABB is a Technology Service that enables the procedure of collecting, storing, and analysing the data produced by the public administration to improve and optimise decisions and performance. Source: Inspired in Gartner Source reference: https://www.gartner.com/en/information-technology/glossary/business-intelligence-bi Example: The following implementation is an example on how this specific Architecture Building Block (ABB) can be instantiated as a Solution Building Block (SBB): Central banks increasingly need to use business intelligence (BI) systems to collect, manage and analyse data in order to inform policy decisions. This report presents the results of a survey conducted by the BIS’s Irving Fisher Committee on Central Bank Statistics (IFC) in 2019.
